ABOUT ENSI

ENSI, founded in 1984, as the first engineering school in Tunisia dedicated to computer sciences, is celebrating its fortieth anniversary this year.

ENSI vision is “to be a “Grande Ecole” of excellence dedicated to computer sciences ranked among the best schools throughout the world", with the mission of “educating global citizens (engineers, researchers) with critical thinking and leaders in responsible digital innovations”.

Its fundamental and shared common core values are E. N. S. I. C. I., refereeing respectively to Excellence, Networking, Sustainability, Innovation in addition to Citizenship and Inclusion. In fact, believing that Excellence have no limits and can never been achieved individually, ENSI is committed to reach better positions and outcomes through multi-level and multi-scale Networking. Also, ENSI is very engaged for local as well as global Sustainability by continuing its noble missions at an international level through contributing to Innovations in processes as well “products”.

ENSI delivers the following NATIONAL DIPLOMA

National Engineering Diploma in Computer Science Engineering
Research Master in Computer Science (MeRSI)
Joint Research Master in BioInformatics
Doctorate in computer science
“Habilitation Universitaire” in Computer Science

About THE EVENT: ENSI Research & Innovation Days

As part of the celebration activities of the 40th anniversary of ENSI, the ENSI Research & Innovation Days are planned to gather together all researchers affiliated to ENSI or to any of its four research structures as well as their team members. This event will be also open to welcoming national and international colleagues and partners.

The ultimate challenging objective of these days is to enhance the connections among all existing networks of people, knowledge and their socio-economic-environmental ecosystems in order to lead to Impactful, Responsible Seamless Research, Innovation & Development sustainable and resilient chains!

Thus, by focusing on the main ENSI researchers achievements, this specific objectives are :
• Promote networking and team building of all researchers affiliated to ENSI as well as its research structures (three big research laboratories and one research units) at a local level, and with researchers all over Tunisia and worldwide,
• Promote exchange of researchers and industrial experts
• Stimulate impactful research through the Valorization of research results through valorization of innovations

This first edition of “ENSI Research and Innovation Days” will begin with a special focus on the historical and pioneering role of ENSI in Teaching and Research of Artificial Intelligence since its foundation in 1984.
